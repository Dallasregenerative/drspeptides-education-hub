# Best Peptides for Weight Loss: Complete Guide

## Top 5 Weight Loss Peptides

### 1. Semaglutide (Ozempic, Wegovy)

**Mechanism:** GLP-1 receptor agonist

**Weight Loss:** 15-17% of body weight

**Key Benefits:**
- FDA-approved for weight loss
- Reduces appetite significantly
- Improves metabolic health
- Cardiovascular benefits

**Dosing:** 0.25mg to 2.4mg weekly

**Best For:** Significant weight loss with proven track record

### 2. Tirzepatide (Mounjaro, Zepbound)

**Mechanism:** Dual GLP-1/GIP agonist

**Weight Loss:** 20-22% of body weight

**Key Benefits:**
- Superior weight loss vs semaglutide
- Better muscle preservation
- Enhanced metabolic effects
- Lower nausea rates

**Dosing:** 2.5mg to 15mg weekly

**Best For:** Maximum weight loss results

### 3. AOD-9604

**Mechanism:** Modified growth hormone fragment

**Weight Loss:** 2-5% of body weight

**Key Benefits:**
- Targets fat metabolism specifically
- No effect on blood sugar
- Preserves muscle mass
- Minimal side effects

**Dosing:** 300mcg daily, subcutaneous

**Best For:** Targeted fat loss without appetite suppression

### 4. CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin

**Mechanism:** Growth hormone secretagogues

**Weight Loss:** 3-7% of body weight

**Key Benefits:**
- Increases lean muscle mass
- Enhances fat metabolism
- Improves body composition
- Better sleep and recovery

**Dosing:** CJC-1295 (2mg weekly) + Ipamorelin (200-300mcg daily)

**Best For:** Body recomposition (fat loss + muscle gain)

### 5. MOTS-c

**Mechanism:** Mitochondrial-derived peptide

**Weight Loss:** 2-4% of body weight

**Key Benefits:**
- Enhances metabolic function
- Improves insulin sensitivity
- Increases energy expenditure
- Anti-aging effects

**Dosing:** 5-10mg twice weekly

**Best For:** Metabolic optimization and longevity
