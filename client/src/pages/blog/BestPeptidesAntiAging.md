# Best Peptides for Anti-Aging: Longevity and Rejuvenation Guide

## Top 7 Anti-Aging Peptides

### 1. Epitalon

**Mechanism:** Telomerase activator, pineal gland regulator

**Key Benefits:**
- Lengthens telomeres
- Regulates circadian rhythm
- Enhances melatonin production
- Neuroprotective effects
- Potential lifespan extension

**Dosing:** 5-10mg per cycle (10-20 days), 2-4 cycles per year

**Best For:** Cellular aging, longevity optimization

### 2. GHK-Cu (Copper Peptide)

**Mechanism:** Tissue remodeling, antioxidant, gene regulation

**Key Benefits:**
- Skin rejuvenation and collagen production
- Wound healing
- Anti-inflammatory
- Hair growth
- Tissue repair

**Dosing:** 
- Injectable: 1-2mg 2-3x/week
- Topical: 0.5-2% concentration

**Best For:** Skin aging, aesthetic anti-aging

### 3. Thymosin Alpha-1

**Mechanism:** Immune system modulator

**Key Benefits:**
- Enhanced immune function
- Increased T-cell production
- Anti-viral properties
- Reduced inflammation
- Cancer prevention support

**Dosing:** 1.6mg 2-3x/week

**Best For:** Immune aging, chronic infections

### 4. SS-31 (Elamipretide)

**Mechanism:** Mitochondrial-targeting peptide

**Key Benefits:**
- Mitochondrial protection
- Enhanced cellular energy
- Cardioprotective effects
- Neuroprotective properties
- Reduced oxidative stress

**Dosing:** 2-5mg daily, subcutaneous

**Best For:** Mitochondrial aging, energy decline

### 5. NAD+ Boosters (NMN, NR peptides)

**Mechanism:** NAD+ precursor supplementation

**Key Benefits:**
- Cellular energy production
- DNA repair
- Sirtu in activation
- Metabolic health
- Cognitive function

**Dosing:** Varies by formulation

**Best For:** Metabolic aging, energy optimization

### 6. Humanin

**Mechanism:** Mitochondrial-derived peptide

**Key Benefits:**
- Neuroprotection
- Metabolic regulation
- Alzheimer's prevention
- Insulin sensitivity
- Lifespan extension (animal studies)

**Dosing:** 2-5mg 2-3x/week

**Best For:** Cognitive aging, metabolic health

### 7. Semax/Selank

**Mechanism:** Neuropeptides, BDNF enhancement

**Key Benefits:**
- Cognitive enhancement
- Neuroprotection
- Stress reduction
- Memory improvement
- Mood regulation

**Dosing:**
- Semax: 300-600mcg daily (nasal)
- Selank: 300-600mcg daily (nasal)

**Best For:** Cognitive aging, brain health

## Safety and Monitoring

**Recommended Monitoring:**
- Baseline and periodic bloodwork
- Telomere length testing (optional)
- Biological age markers
- Functional assessments

**Contraindications:**
- Active cancer (some peptides)
- Pregnancy/breastfeeding
- Autoimmune conditions (some peptides)
