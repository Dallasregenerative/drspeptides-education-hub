## What is NNMT and What Does It Do?

NNMT is an enzyme that is highly active in your adipose (fat) tissue. Think of it as the “master storage switch” for your fat cells. When NNMT activity is high, it sends a powerful signal to your fat cells to do one thing: store fat. It makes them incredibly efficient at soaking up excess calories from your bloodstream and locking them away as triglycerides.

High NNMT activity is associated with:

-   **Increased fat storage**
-   **Decreased metabolic rate**
-   **Obesity**
-   **Type 2 Diabetes**
-   **Fatty Liver Disease**

In essence, NNMT puts your body in a state of extreme metabolic inefficiency, where it is primed to store energy rather than burn it.

## The NNMT and NAD+ Connection

To make matters worse, the NNMT enzyme consumes a vital molecule in its reaction: **NAD+**. NAD+ is a critical coenzyme that is essential for mitochondrial function, energy production, and the activity of sirtuins, the so-called “longevity genes.”

When NNMT is overactive, it acts like a sponge, soaking up the available NAD+ in your cells. This creates a vicious cycle: high NNMT leads to low NAD+, which leads to poor mitochondrial function and a lower metabolic rate, which in turn makes it even easier to store fat.

## Can You Turn Off the Storage Switch?

This raises a tantalizing question: what if you could inhibit the NNMT enzyme? Could you flip the switch on your fat cells from “storage mode” to “burning mode”?

This is the exciting science behind a new class of compounds known as NNMT inhibitors. The most well-known of these is **5-Amino-1MQ**. This small molecule is designed to enter fat cells and directly block the activity of the NNMT enzyme.

The effects are profound:

1.  **It promotes lipolysis**, the release of stored fat from your fat cells.
2.  **It increases cellular NAD+ levels**, providing more fuel for your mitochondria and boosting your metabolic rate.
3.  **It can induce the “browning” of white fat**, making it more metabolically active and thermogenic.

By inhibiting NNMT, 5-Amino-1MQ effectively reprograms your fat cells to burn energy instead of storing it.
