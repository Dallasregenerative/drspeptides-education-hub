## The Old Dogma: You Can’t Grow New Brain Cells

For decades, the prevailing scientific wisdom was that the brain you were born with was the brain you were stuck with. We believed that once brain cells died, they were gone forever. But over the past few decades, this dogma has been shattered by the discovery of **neurogenesis** and **synaptogenesis**—the brain’s incredible ability to grow new neurons and form new connections throughout our lives.

This is the key to true, long-lasting cognitive enhancement. Instead of just temporarily boosting the performance of your existing neural hardware, what if you could physically upgrade it?
