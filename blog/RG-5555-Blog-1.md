All healing begins in the gut. A “leaky gut,” where the intestinal barrier is compromised, allows inflammatory molecules to flood your system, creating a state of chronic, low-grade inflammation. This systemic fire diverts your body’s resources away from targeted healing and keeps you in a pro-inflammatory state. **Larazotide** is a unique peptide that acts as a zonulin antagonist, effectively “sealing” the tight junctions in your gut. This is the critical first step: it calms the systemic inflammation, freeing up your body’s healing capacity.

## Step 2: Extinguish the Local Fire (KPV)

With the systemic fire under control, the next step is to address the inflammation at the specific site of injury. **KPV** is one of the most potent anti-inflammatory peptides known. It works by inhibiting NF-κB, the master switch for the inflammatory response within your cells. This action stops the production of destructive inflammatory cytokines, reducing pain and swelling and creating a clean, low-inflammation environment that is conducive to healing.

It ensures that the necessary cellular building blocks for repair are rapidly delivered to the precise location where they are needed.

## Step 4: Rebuild the Structure (BPC-157)

With the workers on site, the final step is to provide the master blueprint for reconstruction. **BPC-157** is a legendary healing peptide that orchestrates the final phase of tissue regeneration. It stimulates angiogenesis (the formation of new blood vessels) to supply the site with oxygen and nutrients, and it directly promotes the growth and repair of tendon, ligament, muscle, and bone. It is the ultimate catalyst for rebuilding stronger, more resilient tissue.
